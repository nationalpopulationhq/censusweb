Prepare and maintain a National Geographic Frame for the delineation exercise for Censuses and Surveys!

One of the mandates of the National Population Commission is to: Prepare and maintain a National Geographic Frame for the delineation exercise for Censuses and Surveys. To achieve this mandate, the Cartography Department worked assiduously over the years to develop a Sustainable National Geographic Frame that is in tandem with international Standards. This was achieved through Enumeration Area Demarcation (EAD). EAD is the division of the entire country into smaller units determined by standardized criteria which includes population threshold, terrain difficulty, distance etc, with clearly defined boundaries in such a manner that there is no overlap or gap between them. One of the main products of the EAD is the enumerators assignment map which helps to determine enumerator's workload during a Census or Survey.

Year 2023 Population and Housing Census EAD Objectives

The EAD for the Year 2023 had the following objectives

  • To develop a GIS based methodology which takes into consideration the Settlement and Terrain peculiarities as well as socio-economic variables of the Nation.

  • To develop a Sustainable and Internationally acceptable National Geographic Frame which coincides with the Nigerian Administrative boundaries (especially the fourth administrative level - the RA/Ward) for Censuses and Surveys.

  • To generate a unique coding system for EAD entities.

  • To ensure that the entire country is divided into seamless and contiguous Enumeration Areas without gaps or overlaps.
